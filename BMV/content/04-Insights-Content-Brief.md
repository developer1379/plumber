# Insights Content Brief — 6 Launch Articles

`/insights/[slug]/` — blog-style, shorter, more voice-driven and
topical than Guides. This is where Rob's personality and local
knowledge come through most directly (first-person where natural,
matching the Marley "first-plural/named individual" voice pattern but
scaled to a solo operator, so first-person singular fits better here
than Marley's "we"). Each ships with `BlogPosting` schema
(`author: { @id: #person }`, `publisher: { @id: #organization }`).

Rotation cadence recommendation (matches the proven Marley cadence):
after the 6 launch pieces, add 1 new Insights/Guides/FAQ page per day,
rotating — not a burst of new content every day, which risks thin
content on a small site.

## 1. Why Your Boiler Makes Strange Noises (And When It's Serious)

- **Slug:** `boiler-making-noises-when-to-worry`
- **Target keywords:** boiler making noise, boiler banging, boiler gurgling, kettling boiler
- **Angle:** Rob talks through the common noises (kettling, banging,
  gurgling, whistling) and what each usually means, ending with a clear
  "call us" trigger for the genuinely urgent ones (banging/kettling
  from a scaled-up heat exchanger; strong smell alongside noise = stop
  and call immediately).
- **Internal links:** boiler service page, emergency callouts page.
- **Word count:** 500-700.
