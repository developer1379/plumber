# Guides Content Brief — 6 Launch Articles

`/guides/[slug]/` — evergreen, comprehensive, pillar-style reference
content (longer and more structured than Insights). These are the
pages most likely to earn AI-answer-engine citations (see the AEO
stack in `02-Technical-SEO-Spec.md`) because they answer a full
question thoroughly rather than taking an angle. Each ships with
`BlogPosting`/`Article` schema plus `FAQPage` schema for any embedded
Q&A section, and should be the primary internal-link target from the
matching Service page and relevant FAQs.
